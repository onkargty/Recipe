Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a crispy fermented rice-and-lentil crepe filled with spiced potato masala. A South Indian favorite.

Ingredients

  • Dosa batter (rice + urad dal ferment)
  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric, green chilli

Method

  1. Prepare potato masala: temper mustard seeds, saut√© onion and spices, mix with potatoes.
  2. Spread batter thin on hot griddle, cook until crisp, add potato filling and fold.
  3. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
