Veg Biryani

Veg Biryani is a fragrant layered rice dish cooked with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices. Perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • Yogurt, saffron (optional), salt

Method

  1. Par-cook rice and set aside. Saut√© vegetables and spices in oil.
  2. Layer rice and vegetables, add saffron milk, then cook covered on low heat for 15 minutes.
  3. Fluff before serving; pair with raita.
