Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is a spicy mashed vegetable curry served with soft buttered buns (pav). A popular Mumbai street food.

Ingredients

  • Mixed vegetables (potato, peas, carrot)
  • Pav bhaji masala
  • Butter, onion, tomato
  • Buns (pav) to serve

Method

  1. Boil and mash vegetables. Saut√© onion and tomato, add spices and mashed veggies.
  2. Cook until thick and well blended; finish with butter and chopped coriander.
  3. Toast pav in butter and serve with bhaji, onions and lemon.
