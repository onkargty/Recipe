Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich, creamy curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) simmered in a spiced tomato-butter sauce.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices: garam masala, red chilli, turmeric, salt

Method

  1. Sauté ginger-garlic in butter, add tomato puree and spices.
  2. Cook until oil separates, add paneer cubes and simmer 5–7 minutes.
  3. Garnish with cream and serve hot with naan or rice.
