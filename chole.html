Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature pairs spicy chickpea curry (chole) with deep-fried fluffy bread (bhature). A hearty North Indian meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • Onion, tomato, ginger-garlic paste
  • Chole masala, red chilli, salt
  • Ingredients for bhature: maida, yogurt, baking powder

Method

  1. Cook chickpeas until tender. Saut√© onions, tomatoes and spices; add chickpeas and simmer.
  2. Prepare bhature dough, roll and deep fry until puffed and golden.
  3. Serve hot with pickles and sliced onions.
