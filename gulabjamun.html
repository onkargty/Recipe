Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun are soft milk-solid dumplings soaked in a fragrant sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 2 tbsp flour, 1 tbsp ghee
  • Sugar, water, cardamom for syrup

Method

  1. Make a soft dough from milk powder, flour and ghee; shape small balls.
  2. Fry gently until golden; soak in warm sugar syrup flavored with cardamom.
  3. Serve warm or at room temperature.
